Woman yelling at cat meme and its origin

Published on May 29, 2023

What is the Woman yelling at cat meme?

The "Woman Yelling at a Cat" meme originated from a screenshot of a scene in the reality TV show The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills. The image captures Taylor Armstrong and Kyle Richards engaged in a heated argument, while in a separate frame, a perplexed-looking cat sits behind a dinner plate. This meme format gained immense popularity on the internet in mid-June 2019 and has been widely shared and remixed since. The cat in the image was later identified as Smudge the Cat, adding to the meme's enduring appeal.

Smudge the cat

Smudge the Cat, known by his Instagram handle @smudge_lord, is the feline star of the meme. He gained popularity under the nickname "Table Cat" due to his appearance in a photograph where he is seated in front of a salad plate, which became part of the well-known Woman Yelling at a Cat meme. The meme, also referred to as Confused Cat at Dinner, features Smudge's perplexed expression as he looks at the camera. As a white cat, Smudge rose to online fame for his role in this meme, capturing the hearts of many internet users with his memorable presence
